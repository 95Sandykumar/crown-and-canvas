## Frequently Asked Questions

**What photo should I use to order a pet portrait gift?**
Use a clear, well-lit photo where the pet is facing forward and their eyes are visible. Natural light works best. You don't need a professional photo. A good smartphone shot taken outdoors is usually perfect. Avoid heavily filtered images, as they can change the pet's true coloring.

**How long does it take to receive the finished portrait?**
Digital downloads are delivered within 24 to 48 hours after your order is placed. Premium canvas and framed prints ship within 5 to 7 business days. Rush processing is available at checkout if you need it sooner.

**Can I order a portrait of someone else's pet as a gift?**
Yes. You just need a clear photo of their pet. You can find one on their social media or ask a mutual friend. The finished portrait is delivered to you, so you decide when the recipient sees it. It works as a complete surprise every time.
