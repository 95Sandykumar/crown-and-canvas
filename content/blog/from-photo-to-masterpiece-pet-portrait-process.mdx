Ever wonder what happens after you upload your pet's photo to [Crown & Canvas](/)? The journey from a simple snapshot to a majestic royal portrait involves a fascinating blend of technology, artistry, and quality control. Here's a behind-the-scenes look at our process.

## Step 1: Upload Your Pet's Photo

Everything starts with a great photo. When you [start your order](/order/upload), you'll upload a clear image of your pet. Here's what makes a great source photo:

- **Good lighting** - Natural light works best, avoid harsh shadows
- **Clear face** - Make sure your pet's face is clearly visible and in focus
- **Front or three-quarter angle** - These angles produce the most striking portraits
- **High resolution** - The more detail in the photo, the better the final result

Don't worry if your photo isn't studio-quality. Our AI is trained to work with everyday pet photos, including those slightly blurry action shots of your dog catching a frisbee.

## Step 2: Choose Your Style

This is the fun part. Browse our [gallery of portrait styles](/styles) and pick the one that best represents your pet's personality. We offer five categories:

### Renaissance Collection
Classic oil painting styles inspired by the great masters. Perfect for pets with a dignified air. Popular choices include [The Renaissance King](/styles/renaissance-king) and [The Scholar](/styles/renaissance-scholar).

### Military Collection
Distinguished military uniforms and heroic poses. Ideal for brave, loyal pets. [The General](/styles/military-general) is a fan favorite.

### Royalty Collection
Imperial grandeur fit for the most regal of pets. From [The Emperor](/styles/royalty-emperor) to [The Princess](/styles/royalty-princess), these styles exude power and grace.

### Fantasy Collection
For pets with a magical personality. Wizards, knights, and dragon riders bring an element of adventure.

### Modern Collection
Contemporary fun with styles like [The Astronaut](/styles/modern-astronaut) and The Superhero. Great for pets with larger-than-life personalities.

## Step 3: Customize Your Order

Once you've picked a style, you can customize your order:

- **Name your character** - Give your pet a royal title (e.g., "King Biscuit, Ruler of the Couch")
- **Choose your format** - Digital download, premium canvas, or framed print
- **Select your size** - From desktop-friendly to wall-dominating
- **Add extras** - Gift wrapping, rush processing, and more

## Step 4: AI Portrait Generation

This is where the magic happens. Our AI engine:

1. **Analyzes your pet's features** - facial structure, fur patterns, coloring, and expression
2. **Maps to the chosen style** - Integrating your pet's likeness into the historical costume and setting
3. **Generates the portrait** - Creating a high-resolution artwork that preserves your pet's unique personality
4. **Refines the details** - Multiple passes for costume accuracy, lighting, and compositional balance

The entire generation process is optimized to deliver your digital proof within 24-48 hours.

## Step 5: Quality Review

Every portrait goes through a quality review before delivery. We check for:

- **Likeness accuracy** - Does the portrait look like your pet?
- **Style consistency** - Are the historical details accurate?
- **Technical quality** - Resolution, color balance, and overall polish
- **The "wow" factor** - Does it make you smile?

## Step 6: Delivery

Depending on your chosen format:

- **Digital Downloads** - Delivered to your email within 24-48 hours
- **Canvas Prints** - Printed on premium cotton canvas, gallery-wrapped on a solid wood frame, shipped within 5-7 business days
- **Framed Prints** - Museum-quality archival paper in a premium frame, shipped within 5-7 business days

All physical products ship with protective packaging and tracking information.
